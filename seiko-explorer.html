The Seiko Explorer

[CLIENT BUILD] Based on the already legendary Seiko NH35 automatic movement, this timepiece is ready for any adventure you throw at it.

The timeless appeal for a field watch, ready for rugged adventures and sophisticated soirées.

These are not just instruments of timekeeping, but symbols of a legacy that champions resilience, functionality, and style.

This Explorer homage, like the original, is so simple, yet so gorgeous. The beauty lies in its simplicity – only hands and markers, with 3, 6, and 9 hours in lettering. The beloved and highly legible Mercedes hands and markers come equipped with blue lume.

Based on the reliable and precise Seiko NH35 automatic movement, this timepiece is ready for any adventure you throw at it.

  • Movement Seiko NH35
  • Case size / thickness 36mm / 11.9mm (including crystal)
  • Material 316L
  • Glass Scratch-resistant sapphire crystal
