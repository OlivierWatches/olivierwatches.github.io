The Tony - A Gold Datejust

[CLIENT BUILD] The desire of gold is not for gold. It is the desire for freedom and benefit.

An homage to the champagne gold Rolex Daydate that Tony Soprano wears on the famed show, The Sopranos.

Whattaya hear, whattaya say?

Marone! The dial is even more beautiful in person, and pictures can't do it justice. The fluted pattern, the same style as the bezel, plays under the lights beautifully. We call it the 'pasta fazool' pattern in this case. The president steel bracelet is the ultimate symbol of comfort and class and has a hidden clasp that adds to the minimalistic look of this timepiece.

Based on the reliable and precise Seiko NH35 automatic movement, this timepiece isn’t going to stop ticking easily.

  • Movement Seiko NH35
  • Case size / thickness 39mm / 11.9mm (including crystal)
  • Material 316L
  • Glass Scratch-resistant sapphire crystal with cyclops and AR coating
