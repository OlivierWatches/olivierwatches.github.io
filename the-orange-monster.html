The Orange Monster

A black & orange Yachtmaster homage.

This Yachtmaster build is fun, sporty, and all-black – even the date is in black. However, its darkness breaks with bold orange details.

The stealth black unidirectional rotating bezel offers a timer feature - a trademark of diver watches. Simply turn the marker to the minute hand and follow the progressing time through the bezel.

Hands come equipped with C3 Super-LumiNova Lume in green, the brightest of all lume colors.

The orange-colored, curved FKM rubber strap with a black tang buckle ensures the ideal balance between comfort and style.

Based on the reliable and precise Seiko NH35 automatic movement, this timepiece isn’t going to stop ticking easily.

  • Movement Seiko NH35
  • Case size / thickness 40mm / 13.5mm (including crystal)
  • Material 316L stainless steel and ceramic bezel
  • Glass Scratch-resistant sapphire crystal with cyclops and blue AR
