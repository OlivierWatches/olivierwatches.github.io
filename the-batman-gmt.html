The Batman GMT

“It's not who you are underneath, it's what you do that defines you.”

This Batman homage is classy and sporty at the same time, and pairs well with literally anything, which makes it a perfect daily watch.

This timepiece has a blue 24-hour GMT hand that rotates one full rotation every 24 hours, and is used to follow local time. The black and blue unidirectional rotating bezel offers a GMT feature that will allow you to read the time in an additional time zone.

Hands and markers come equipped with C3 Super-LumiNova Lume in green, the brightest of all lume colors.

The brushed 316L-grade steel bracelet ensures the ideal balance between comfort and style for an everyday watch.

Based on the reliable and precise Seiko NH34 automatic GMT movement, this timepiece isn’t going to stop ticking easily, and it allows you to track different time zones at the same time.

  • Movement Seiko NH34 GMT
  • Case size / thickness 40mm / 13.5mm (including crystal)
  • Material 316L stainless steel and ceramic bezel
  • Glass Scratch-resistant sapphire crystal with cyclops
