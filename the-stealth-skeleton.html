The Stealth Skeleton

Stealth is seeing what everybody has seen and thinking what nobody has thought. And it comes in rose gold this time.

Powered by the Seiko NH70

Reliable, precise, AND skeletonized automatic movement.

This yachmaster homage is bold and flashy, yet elegant, and comes in 40mm, for most a perfect size.

The stealth black unidirectional rotating bezel offers a timer feature - a trademark of diver watches. Simply rotate the marker to the minute hand and follow the progressing time through the bezel. Hands come equipped with C3 Super-LumiNova Lume in green, the brightest of all lume colors, so you won’t miss those when in stealth mode.

The high-quality FKM rubber strap from Monstraps in black with rose gold tang buckle ensures the ideal balance between comfort and style - it’s suitable for any occasion.

Based on the reliable and precise Seiko NH70 skeletonized automatic movement, this timepiece won’t stop ticking easily, and you’ll be able to take a look at the movement at all times.

  • Movement Seiko NH70
  • Case size / thickness 40mm / 11.9mm (including crystal)
  • Material 316L stainless steel and ceramic bezel
  • Glass Scratch-resistant sapphire crystal with AR coating on both sides
